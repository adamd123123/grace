About Grace

Since 2002

Grace Holland embodies the essence of vibrancy and compassion in every facet of her life. Born and raised amidst the scenic landscapes of Provo, Utah, Grace's early years were shaped by a close-knit community and a love for adventure. From an early age, she displayed an innate curiosity and a boundless energy, always eager to explore new opportunities and forge meaningful connections with those around her.

As she transitioned into adolescence, Grace's family relocated to Orem, Utah, where she continued to blossom into a remarkable young woman. With a passion for tennis, volleyball and a penchant for social gatherings, Grace became a fixture in her community, known for her infectious laughter and unwavering kindness. Her love for sports and camaraderie blended seamlessly with her dedication to academic excellence, creating a well-rounded foundation for her future endeavors.

Grace Holland's journey began in Utah before she moved to North Carolina for high school. After a year at Brigham Young University, she answered the call to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Her dedication to sharing the gospel touched many lives in Bakersfield, California, and Brisbane, Australia. Today, Grace continues to inspire others with her unwavering devotion to spreading messages of hope and compassion, embodying the spirit of service and faith wherever she goes.

Her path from her mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints to Brigham Young University was marked by a captivating light that drew people to her. It was at BYU that Adam DeMille, enchanted by Grace's kindness and inner strength, found himself mesmerized by her luminous presence. Their love story began with a chance blind date, where Adam was immediately struck by Grace's warmth and genuine personality, as they bonded over their shared love for simple pleasures like indulging in 99 cent corndogs from Sonic.

As they embarked on their journey together, Adam found himself continually inspired by Grace's unwavering positivity and her ability to brighten any room with her infectious laughter. Their shared experiences, from enjoying simple pleasures to embarking on new adventures, deepened their connection and strengthened their bond. After a year filled with moments of joy and laughter, Adam eagerly anticipates their upcoming wedding in June of 2024, knowing that he has found in Grace a partner whose light will guide them through every step of their journey together.
